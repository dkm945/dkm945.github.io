I am an aspiring web developer with a lifelong love of technology. Somehow I never thought to try and make a livelihood from it until after I got a Master's degree in music. The first time I finagled with computers was in the mid-90's. I spent an inordinate amount of time looking through the Warcraft II CD and its section in Program Files (or whatever it was called at the time) to see what I could open, and I remember opening some MIDI files in the audio player. However, I ended up messing up some of the file associations somehow and couldn't fix it. I left it alone since it didn't affect the game and no one else in the family would notice.

When I'm not studying programming my fondness for technology extends to video games and finagling elsewhere. With respect to gaming, I usually play Destiny 2 with my clanmates in pursuit of "Optimal DPS." Additionally, I help improve and configure our clan's Discord server with the help of Charlemagne and Carl-bot.
